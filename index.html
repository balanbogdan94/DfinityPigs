Our story

The @Wolf surrounded the pigs's house and tried to destroy it from the ground with his strong breath. The 3 pigs trembled with fear and looked for a way to get out of the mess created by the evil beast. One of them saw a book behind the fireplace, a book he had never seen before and discovers in it a magical portal into the multivers WHICH took them to the World of DfinityPigs. But they find themselves in a world ruled by 10 evil @Wolf s. The three decide to hide and analyze this new world. They discover here a lot of pigs, of all ages and types, pigs with shabby clothes, which serve the 10 wolves. The three DECIDE to do something against the wolves that ruled over the World of the DfinityPigs. Together with the slaves they used their senses to drive them off the throne and lock them in safe prisons, where they can no longer harm them. Now peace is being restored in the land.
